Cookies Policy

Learn about the cookies we use, how they affect your experience, and your choices regarding them.

Introduction

At Irriguide Pro, we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and provide personalized content and ads. This Cookies Policy explains how we collect and use cookie data and outlines your rights and choices.

Detailed Contact-Related Points

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience. Cookies enable functions such as remembering your login, language settings, and other preferences.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use different types of cookies: strictly necessary, performance, functionality, and targeting cookies. Strictly necessary cookies are essential for site operation, while targeting cookies enable personalized marketing.

3. How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us understand how visitors use our site, measure advertising effectiveness, and customize your experience. For example, cookies record which pages you visit most frequently and help us identify areas for improvement.

4. First-Party vs Third-Party Cookies

First-party cookies are set by Irriguide Pro, while third-party cookies come from external partners or providers. We use third-party services like analytics and advertising networks that may set their own cookies on your device.

5. Your Cookie Choices

You can manage cookie preferences through the opt-in dialog below or your browser settings. You may choose to accept all, decline non-essential cookies, or customize preferences. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

6. How to Manage Cookies in Your Browser

All major browsers let you control cookie settings through preferences or privacy controls. You can delete existing cookies, block third-party cookies, or restrict all cookies. For detailed instructions, visit your specific browser's help section.

7. Cookie Duration and Expiry

Cookies we use can be session-based (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent (stored for a set time). Persistent cookies improve convenience by remembering your settings for future visits.

8. Data Collected by Cookies

Cookies collect data such as your IP address, browser type, pages viewed, time spent, and interactions on site. We use aggregated data for analytics and improve website performance while respecting your privacy.

9. Cookies and Your Privacy Rights

Your cookie data is handled consistent with our Privacy Policy and applicable data protection laws. You can exercise rights to access, modify or delete data collected via cookies as outlined there.

10. How Cookies Enhance Site Security

Certain cookies help protect against fraudulent activity and unauthorized access to accounts. Security cookies detect anomalies and help maintain a safe browsing environment.

11. Cookies for Personalized Content

We use cookies to tailor site content and ads to your interests, improving relevance and user experience. This involves tracking your interactions and preferences over time.

12. Advertising Cookies

Advertising cookies enable us and our partners to serve ads based on your interests and measure their effectiveness. These cookies are subject to our partners’ policies.

13. Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies collect data about website usage to help us understand user behavior and improve content and design. Data may include pages visited, time on site, and device type.

14. Functionality Cookies

Functionality cookies remember choices like language or region and provide enhanced features. These help us deliver a more personalized experience.

15. Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are necessary to provide core services such as login authentication and secure access. They cannot be disabled without affecting functionality.

16. Cookies in Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for analytics and ads, which may set cookies and collect data according to their own policies. Examples include Google Analytics and social media platforms.

17. Your Consent

We obtain your consent for non-essential cookies via the cookie banner. You can change or withdraw consent at any time in the cookies preferences or browser settings.

18. Revoking Consent

If you revoke consent, non-essential cookies will be disabled on your device for this site. Please note this may reduce website functionality.

19. Cookies on Mobile Devices

Cookies function similarly on mobile devices, helping optimize your experience on phones or tablets. Browser and app settings control cookie behavior on these devices.

20. Cookies and Browser Security

Cookies only store simple data and cannot execute code or install malware. They are generally safe but should be managed prudently.

21. How to Delete Cookies

You may delete cookies at any time through your browser settings or tools. This removes stored preferences and may require reconfiguration upon next visit.

22. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may limit site functionality, impair login sessions, and degrade user experience. We recommend enabling cookies for full feature access.

23. International Visitors

Cookies and data collection may vary by country depending on local laws. We comply with applicable regulations in jurisdictions where we operate.

24. Cookies for Site Optimization

We use cookies to test and improve site layout, navigation speed, and content delivery. Usage data help us make informed updates.

25. Cookie Duration Examples

Some cookies expire after minutes (session cookies), others may persist for months or years depending on purpose. Persistent cookies balance convenience with data minimization principles.

26. Social Media Cookies

Our site may include social media buttons that set cookies enabling sharing and interaction. These cookies are controlled by the social networks and subject to their policies.

27. Opt-Out Mechanisms

You may opt out of targeted advertising cookies through third-party services such as the Network Advertising Initiative. Links to opt-out pages are often provided in privacy dashboards.

28. Cookie Policy Changes

We review and update this Cookies Policy regularly. Important changes will be communicated via our website notifications or email where applicable.

29. Contact About Cookies

For questions or concerns regarding our cookies or data practices, please contact us using the information in the footer below.

30. Importance of Cookies for Service Delivery

Cookies enable essential functions such as session management, preventing fraud, and maintaining security. These functions protect both users and service providers.

31. Cookie Settings Preference

The cookie consent dialog allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. Your preferences are saved and respected on future visits.

32. Legacy Cookies

Some cookies may be categorized as legacy due to older standards. We are working to retire outdated cookies in favor of modern privacy-respecting methods.

33. Data Minimization Practice

We minimize data collected through cookies to only what is necessary for service improvement and compliance. Personal data collection is limited and protected.

34. Security Measures for Cookie Data

Cookies transmitted over secure HTTPS connections to prevent interception. We do not store sensitive personal data in cookies.

35. How Cookies Support Analytics

Analytics cookies aggregate user behavior data helping us understand site performance trends and user satisfaction. We use data for continuous improvement and problem resolution.

36. Behavioral Advertising

Behavioral advertising cookies track your activity to show relevant ads. We use these cookies only with your explicit consent.

37. Transparency and Control

We provide clear information and easy controls for you to manage cookies and online tracking. Our cookie banner and preference center are designed for user-friendly interaction.

38. Educational Purpose of Cookies

Cookies also help us deliver educational content and tutorials related to irrigation efficiency and sustainability that might interest you.

39. Cookies and Third-Party Widgets

Our site may host third-party widgets that use cookies to function, such as video players, chat support, or survey tools. These are subject to their own policies and controls.

40. Retention and Deletion of Cookies

Cookie data retention depends on the type of cookie and purposes served. You can delete cookies manually or through browser automation. We do not retain cookie data longer than necessary for service purposes.
