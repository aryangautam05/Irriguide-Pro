Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to Irriguide Pro. Learn how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Introduction

At Irriguide Pro, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy applies to all information collected through our website, mobile applications, and any related services, sales, marketing, or events.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide through various interactive features such as contact forms, assessment submissions, and feedback forms. Additionally, we automatically collect certain information about your device, browsing actions, and patterns via cookies and similar technologies. This includes IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and other usage statistics to improve and personalize your experience.
